A Little About Myself

My journey into tech has been a little bit of an unconventional one. I have always had a love of teaching and learning and spent most of my career teaching dance and English as a second language. I have a passion for learning languages and I have studied many styles of art through college. My previous opportunity allowed me to bring my teaching and creativity together by giving me a chance to create lessons on digital platforms. It made me realize just how much of a need there is for easily understandable digital design. This is especially so when one needs to transmit ideas and information or even just make things easy to navigate for online users. Because of this, I decided to start learning programming languages with a leaning towards front-end to supplement my design knowledge so that I could start creating visually pleasing yet functional digital designs, websites, and web applications. So here I am now! Ready to share with you the skills I've learned and my contributions to this ever evolving part of the world.
